Website for Caldas da rainha city council

2023 | Website for Caldas da Rainha City Council | This project was developed in an academic context for the Polytechnic Institute of Leiria - ESAD.CR.
This website proposal was created to improve the accessibility and usability of the existing website, as well as to make its design more appealing and functional. Taking into account the issue of accessibility, we created a menu to meet the needs of possible users with different needs.
Prototype developed at Figma: Caldas da Rainha Council
Project developed by : Joana Cardoso and Matilde Domingues
