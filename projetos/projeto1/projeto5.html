fOrgotten art

2023 | “Forgotten Art” (en) or "Arte Esquecida" (pt) | This project was developed in an academic context, for the Polytechnic Institute of Leiria - ESAD.CR.
Our intervention is based on forgotten art, i.e. art that people come across all the time and yet don't pay enough attention to. Even though they know about the different types of art that exist, in their daily lives, most people end up devaluing a lot of the art that surrounds us, and it is because of this that we carried out this intervention project. In this sample, I'm highlighting the graffiti.
Photographs taken in: Viseu and Caldas da Rainha
Project developed by : Joana Cardoso and Matilde
