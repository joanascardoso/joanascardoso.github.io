ROUGE

2023 | Rouge | This project was developed in an academic context, for the Polytechnic Institute of Leiria - ESAD.CR.
The year is 1960 in Paris, France. This video tells the story of an activist secret society that comes together to create a revolution against the current oppressive regime. The story begins when a group of women come together and help each other for a common purpose: to end oppression by praising female power.
Video shot in Caldas da Rainha
Project developed by : Joana Cardoso, Matilde Domingues, Inês Cerqueira, Joana Félix and Rita Silva
