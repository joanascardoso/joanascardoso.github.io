P etMeet

2022/23 | Petmeet | This project was developed in an academic context, for the Polytechnic Institute of Leiria - ESAD.CR.
This mobile application aims to counteract the loneliness of university students who study away from home and miss their furry companions. It's also a great solution for busy owners who, unfortunately, can't give their pets the time and attention they need.
Prototype developed at Figma: PetMeet
Project developed as a group: Joana Cardoso, Matilde Domingues and Inês Cerqueira.
