DocLisboa

2024 | DocLisboa | This project was developed in an academic context, for the Polytechnic Institute of Leiria - ESAD.CR.
This concept for a mobile application for the DocLisboa Festival came about with the aim of improving the festival's communication and making the information and features on the festival's current website more accessible, especially to a younger audience. The proposal has a more experimental design, but one that fits in with the brand's image.
Prototype developed at Figma: DocLisboa
